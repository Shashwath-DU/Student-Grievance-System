Student Grievance System

A student grievance system is vital for ensuring fairness, accountability, and transparency within educational institutions. It provides a formal avenue for students to voice concerns ranging from academic disputes to discrimination, thereby safeguarding their rights and fostering a safe and respectful learning environment. Such a system holds the institution and its staff accountable, promotes open communication, and enables continuous improvement by addressing barriers to learning.
