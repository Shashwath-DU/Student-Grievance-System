STUDENT GRIEVANCE SYSTEM

Objectives of Grievance System

1. To support students who have been deprived by services offered by college.
2. To provide the college officials a clear picture of problems faced by students.
3. To ensure effective solution to the student's grievances with an impartial and fair appproach.

Functions of Grievance System

1. Redressal of Student's grievances to solve their academic and administrative problems.
2. To co-ordinate between students and departments/section to redress the grievances.
3. To guide ways and means to the students to redress their problems.
